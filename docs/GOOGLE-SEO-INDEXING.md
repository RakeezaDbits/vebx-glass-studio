# Google SEO – Sitelinks & Knowledge Panel (Dynamic Bits jaisa result)

Jab koi **"vebx run"** search kare to aap chahti hain ke result **Dynamic Bits** jaisa dikhe:
- Title ke neeche **sitelinks** (Contact, Services, Our Work, etc.)
- Side me **Knowledge Panel** (rating, phone, address, hours)

Yeh sab Google **khud decide** karta hai, lekin aap sahi foundation de sakti hain.

---

## 1. Sitelinks (title ke neeche wale links)

**Kya hai:** Google main result ke neeche 4–6 internal links dikhata hai (Contact Us, Services, etc.).

**Kaise aate hain:**
- Google **automatically** choose karta hai jab:
  - Site ki **structure clear** ho (sitemap, schema, nav)
  - Site pe **traffic / authority** ho
  - **Crawl** sahi se ho raha ho

**Aapne already kya kiya (technical):**
- Sitemap (`/sitemap.xml`) – saari important URLs
- JSON-LD me **WebSite + ItemList** – main pages (Home, Services, Our Work, Contact, etc.) clearly list hain
- Har page pe **canonical + title + description**
- Header/Footer me **clear internal links** (Services, Contact, About, etc.)

**Aur kya karna hai:**
- **Time do** – nayi sites ko sitelinks aane me mahine lag sakte hain
- **Search Console** me site add karo, sitemap submit karo
- **Backlinks** aur **traffic** badhao – jitna zyada legitimate traffic, utna chance ke Google sitelinks dikhaye

---

## 2. Knowledge Panel (side me company info + rating)

**Kya hai:** Right side pe company name, rating (e.g. 4.8), phone, hours, “Suggest an edit” – jaise Dynamic Bits ke saath.

**Kaise aata hai:**  
Ye **Google Business Profile (GBP)** se aata hai. Bina GBP ke panel usually nahi aata.

**Steps:**

1. **Google Business Profile banao**
   - [business.google.com](https://business.google.com) pe jao
   - “Add your business to Google” / “Manage now”
   - **Business name:** vebx.run (ya jo official name hai)
   - **Category:** e.g. “Software company” / “Web development company”
   - **Address:** wahi jo site pe hai (117 S Lexington Street STN 100, Harrisonville, MO 64701)
   - **Phone:** jo contact number use karti ho
   - **Website:** https://vebx.run
   - **Hours** (optional but helpful)

2. **Verify karo**  
   - Postcard / phone / email se verification complete karo.

3. **Profile complete rakho**
   - Logo, short description, services (Mobile App Development, Web Development, etc.) add karo
   - Photos add karo (office, team, work – agar appropriate ho)

4. **Website pe same info rakho**
   - Contact page pe **same** address, phone, email jo GBP me diya
   - Footer / Contact pe clearly dikhe – Google “NAP” (Name, Address, Phone) match karta hai

5. **Reviews**
   - Clients se Google pe review mangna – rating (e.g. 4.8) panel me tab dikh sakti hai
